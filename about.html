Who We Are

The Green Basket is a proudly South African organic food store founded in 2010. We specialize in providing fresh, chemical-free, and sustainably sourced produce directly from local farms. Our mission is to bring healthier food choices to families, schools, and communities across South Africa.

Our Mission

To make organic living affordable, accessible, and enjoyable for everyone. We believe that eating fresh, natural food is the key to a healthier life and a cleaner environment.

Our Vision

To become the leading organic grocery brand in South Africa, inspiring a nationwide shift towards sustainable farming and healthier lifestyles.

Our Values

  • Sustainability Supporting eco-friendly farming methods.
  • Community Partnering with local farmers and artisans.
  • Health Promoting chemical-free and nutritious food.
  • Trust Ensuring transparency in everything we do.

Why Choose Us?

  • 100% certified organic products
  • Direct partnerships with small-scale farmers
  • Affordable pricing with premium quality
  • Wide range of fresh produce, dairy, bakery, and pantry items
  • Home delivery across Kwazulu-Natal

Our Story

What started as a small family-run organic stall at a Johannesburg farmer’s market has now grown into a trusted nationwide brand. Over the years, we’ve built strong relationships with both our farmers and customers, ensuring that every product we deliver is fresh, ethical, and full of goodness.
